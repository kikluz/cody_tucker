About

Otaam is a Toulouse beatmaker, influenced by jazz and soul as well as hip hop and electro.

After training in jazz at various conservatories, he turned to beatmaking, and thus found a way to combine these different influences and to satisfy his thirst for musical eclecticism.

His music skillfully blends a wide variety of musical genres, alternating hypnotic and groovy tracks, in order to best express the wide range of emotions he wishes to convey through his music.

On the decks behind Devi Reed, he also brings a breath of fresh air to reggae by mixing it with hip / hop and electro sounds.
